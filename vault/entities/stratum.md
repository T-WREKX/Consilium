# Stratum (historical codename)

The original codename for [[consilium|Consilium]], used during the early proposal phase. "Stratum" reflected the layered architecture (knowledge accumulating in distinct layers with their own privacy properties). The name changed to **Consilium** before any user-facing material was published — evoking structured growth and the lattice of connections that knowledge graphs actually look like, less formal and more inviting.

## Key facts

- **Do not use Stratum anywhere user-facing.** (see [[consilium-context-dump]])
- The internal architectural mental model is still "stratum" (layered); the product is "Consilium."
- The name change was a deliberate brand decision: Stratum sounded formal/geological; Consilium evokes growth and support.
- Some early draft material used Stratum for headings. Any such artifact is internal history only.
