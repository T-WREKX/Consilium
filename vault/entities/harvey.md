# Harvey

A leading legal-AI product. Used in the Consilium pitch as the **market-validation comp** (Harvey at $3B confirms the space) and as the **archetypal MCP consumer** for [[consilium|Consilium]]'s V1 pluggable-brain story.

## Key facts

- **Reference valuation**: **$3B in 2025** — explicitly cited as the proof point that legal AI is a real, well-funded market. The [[consilium-context-dump|context dump]] names this valuation as one of the reasons legal beat healthcare and defense for the hackathon vertical. (see [[consilium-product-brief]], [[consilium-context-dump]])
- **Category**: legal AI point tool — heavy on contract review, research, drafting workflows.
- **Consilium's positioning**: not a competitor in the strict sense. Consilium is the **firm-specific knowledge substrate that plugs into Harvey**, not against it. Harvey + Consilium-grounded queries become firm-aware in a way Harvey alone cannot. (see [[legal-ai-landscape]])
- **Brand reference**: cited in [[consilium-design-guidelines]] as the **#1 reference point** for "legal credibility and dark, restrained interfaces."
- **V1 integration**: Harvey is the first-listed consumer of the [[mcp-server]] endpoint that exposes the team graph for grounded firm-specific responses.

## Relations

- **Competes positionally with**: Consilium (in mindshare and budget) — but [[consilium]] frames itself as complementary, not substitutional
- **V1 MCP consumer of**: [[mcp-server]]
- **Adjacent legal-AI tools**: [[spellbook]] (drafting), [[cocounsel]] (research)

## Open questions

- Has Harvey published an MCP client roadmap? Consilium's pluggable-brain story depends on Harvey or peers consuming the MCP endpoint.
