# Spellbook

Legal AI point tool focused on **drafting**. Cited in the [[consilium-product-brief|Consilium competitive positioning matrix]] as an example of point-solution legal AI that does not solve the underlying problem: the firm has no living, queryable layer of its own accumulated intelligence.

## Key facts

- **Category**: legal AI point tool — drafting. (see [[consilium-product-brief]])
- **Consilium differentiator**: Consilium is the firm's knowledge substrate that plugs *into* drafting tools, not a competitor to them.
- **V1 MCP consumer**: like [[harvey]], a candidate consumer of the [[mcp-server]] endpoint.

## Relations

- **Adjacent legal-AI tools**: [[harvey]], [[cocounsel]]
- **V1 MCP consumer of**: [[mcp-server]]

## Open questions

- Spellbook's privilege handling and on-prem/cloud posture — relevant to firms that have banned public AI.
