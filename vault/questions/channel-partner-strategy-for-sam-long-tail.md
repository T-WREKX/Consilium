# Channel partner strategy for the SAM long tail — which partners?

## Status

**Open** — awaiting team decision.

## Why it matters

The [[consilium-business-model|business model]] explicitly names partnership channels as required for the bottom of SAM — firms that outsource KM to consultants. The brief names the requirement but does not name partners. Without partners, that slice of SAM is unreachable through the direct motion. (see [[consilium-business-model]], [[buyer-at-firms-without-km-function]])

## What we know so far

- Sales cycles 3–9 months for direct-motion mid-size firms.
- Long-tail firms outsource KM to consulting partners (Bigfork, Helm360, Wilson Allen, HBR Consulting).
- No partner relationships exist or are in development.

## What would resolve it

- Identify 2–3 candidate channel partners.
- Decide whether channel sales is V1 or V2.
- Define the partner economic model (revenue share, referral fee, white-label allowed?).
